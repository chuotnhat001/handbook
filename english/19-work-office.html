Work & Office

Cong viec va van phong — giao tiep noi cong so, hop, email, va cac tinh huong chuyen nghiep

Mau cau co ban

Trong cuoc hop

Let's get started. — Chung ta bat dau nhe.
Can I share my screen? — Toi chia se man hinh duoc khong?
I'd like to add something. — Toi muon bo sung.
Could you repeat that? — Ban noi lai duoc khong?
Let's take this offline. — De minh ban rieng sau.
Any questions before we wrap up? — Co cau hoi gi truoc khi ket thuc khong?

Yeu cau / De nghi

  • Could you send me the report by Friday? — Ban gui bao cao truoc thu 6 duoc khong?
  • Would you mind reviewing this? — Ban xem giup cai nay duoc khong?
  • I'll get back to you on that. — Toi se tra loi ban sau.
  • Let me check and follow up. — De toi kiem tra va cap nhat sau.
  • Can we schedule a quick call? — Minh sap xep cuoc goi nhanh duoc khong?

Bao cao tien do

  • I'm working on [task]. — Toi dang lam [task].
  • It's almost done. — Sap xong roi.
  • I'm blocked on [issue]. — Toi bi ket o [van de].
  • I need more time. — Toi can them thoi gian.
  • I'll have it ready by [time]. — Toi se hoan thanh truoc [thoi gian].
  • I'm ahead of schedule. — Toi di truoc tien do.

Email chuyen nghiep

Mo dau:
Hi [Name], / Dear [Name],
I hope this email finds you well.
Just following up on our conversation...

Ket thuc:
Let me know if you have any questions.
Looking forward to hearing from you.
Best regards, / Kind regards, / Thanks,

So sanh voi tieng Viet

Giong: Ca hai ngon ngu deu co cac cap do trang trong khac nhau tuy tinh huong.

Khac:

  • Tieng Anh dung "Could/Would" de lich su hon "Can". Tieng Viet dung "duoc khong" cho ca hai.
  • Tieng Anh khong co he thong xung ho phuc tap nhu tieng Viet (anh/chi/em/sep). Chi co "you" cho tat ca.
  • Email tieng Anh ngan gon, di thang vao van de. Email tieng Viet thuong co phan chao hoi dai hon.
  • Tieng Anh dung "Let's take this offline" (ban rieng sau) — khong co tuong duong truc tiep trong tieng Viet.
  • "I'll get back to you" la cam ket se tra loi — khong phai loi tu choi lich su nhu "de toi xem" trong tieng Viet.

Dialogue mau

Tinh huong 1: Daily standup

Lead: Alright, let's do a quick round. Minh, you want to go first?

Minh: Sure. Yesterday I finished the API integration for the payment module. Today I'm writing unit tests for it. No blockers.

Lead: Great. Sarah?

Sarah: I'm still working on the dashboard redesign. I'm blocked on the design specs — waiting for the UX team to finalize the mockups.

Lead: I'll ping them after this. Anyone else have blockers?

David: I need access to the staging database. Could someone help me with that?

Lead: I'll sort that out today. Anything else? No? Let's get to it.

Tinh huong 2: Gui email xin nghi phep

Subject: Leave Request — Dec 23-27

Body:

Hi David,

I'd like to request time off from December 23 to 27 for a family trip. I've already coordinated with Sarah to cover any urgent tasks during that time.

Please let me know if this works.

Thanks,
Minh

Tinh huong 3: Hoi y kien trong hop

You: I'd like to suggest a different approach for the migration.

Lead: Sure, go ahead.

You: Instead of doing a big-bang migration over the weekend, we could do it incrementally — one service at a time. It's slower but much less risky.

Lead: That's a good point. What's the timeline look like?

You: About two weeks instead of one, but we avoid downtime and can roll back easily if something goes wrong.

Lead: Let's discuss the tradeoffs with the team and decide by Thursday.

Loi thuong gap cua nguoi Viet

  • Sai: "I have a meeting at 3 hours." → Dung: "I have a meeting at 3 o'clock / at 3 p.m."
  • Sai: "Can you help me do this?" (voi sep) → Dung: "Would you mind helping me with this?" (lich su hon voi cap tren)
  • Sai: "I will finish it in next week." → Dung: "I'll finish it next week." (khong can "in")
  • Sai: "Please revert to me." → Dung: "Please get back to me." hoac "Please reply." (revert = quay lai trang thai cu, khong phai tra loi)
  • Sai: "I'm working in this company 3 years." → Dung: "I've been working at this company for 3 years." (Present Perfect Continuous + for)
  • Sai: "Kindly do the needful." → Dung: "Could you please take care of this?" (cau kia qua cu va khong tu nhien)

Meo giao tiep

  • "Could/Would" luon lich su hon "Can" khi yeu cau — dac biet voi sep hoac khach hang.
  • Email ngan gon: 1 muc dich = 1 email. Khong viet qua 5 cau neu khong can thiet.
  • "Let's take this offline" = "ban rieng sau, khong chiem thoi gian hop".
  • "I'll circle back" = "toi se quay lai van de nay sau" — rat pho bien trong meeting.
  • Standup format: Yesterday / Today / Blockers — giu ngan gon, moi nguoi 30-60 giay.

Bai tap

1. Ban muon nho dong nghiep xem lai code. Noi cau nao lich su nhat?
a) Review my code.
b) Can you review my code?
c) Would you mind reviewing my code?

c) Would you mind reviewing my code? (lich su nhat)

2. Sua loi: "I'm working in this company since 2020."

I've been working at this company since 2020. (Present Perfect Continuous + since)

3. Ban bi ket vi chua co design specs. Bao cao trong standup the nao?

I'm blocked on the design specs — still waiting for the UX team to finalize them.

4. Sua loi: "Please revert to me by end of day."

Please get back to me by end of day. / Please reply by end of day.

5. Dien vao cho trong: "I ___ the report ready ___ tomorrow morning."

I'll have the report ready by tomorrow morning.

6. Trong hop, ban muon bo sung y kien. Noi the nao?

I'd like to add something. / Can I jump in here? / If I may add...

7. Viet email ngan (3-4 cau) xin nghi phep ngay thu 6 tuan nay.

Vi du:
Hi [Manager],
I'd like to take this Friday off for a personal appointment. I've made sure there are no urgent deliverables that day. Please let me know if that works.
Thanks, Minh

8. "Let's take this offline" nghia la gi?
a) Tat may tinh
b) Ban rieng sau, khong chiem thoi gian hop
c) Gui email thay vi noi truc tiep

b) Ban rieng sau, khong chiem thoi gian hop.

9. Sua loi: "I have a meeting at 3 hours afternoon."

I have a meeting at 3 p.m. / at 3 in the afternoon.

10. Viet standup update cua ban hom nay (Yesterday / Today / Blockers).

Vi du:
Yesterday: I completed the user authentication feature and submitted a PR.
Today: I'm starting on the notification system.
Blockers: None at the moment.
