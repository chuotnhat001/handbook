Food & Restaurant

An uong va nha hang — goi mon, hoi ve thuc don, thanh toan

Mau cau co ban

Dat ban / Vao nha hang

I'd like to make a reservation for [number] people.
Do you have a table for two?
I have a reservation under [name].
How long is the wait? — Doi bao lau?

Goi mon

  • Can I see the menu, please? — Cho toi xem thuc don.
  • I'll have the... — Toi goi...
  • I'd like the... — Toi muon goi...
  • What do you recommend? — Ban goi y mon gi?
  • What's today's special? — Mon dac biet hom nay la gi?
  • Can I get this without [ingredient]? — Bo [nguyen lieu] duoc khong?
  • I'm allergic to [food]. — Toi bi di ung voi [thuc an].
  • Is this spicy? — Mon nay cay khong?

Trong bua an

  • Could I have some more water? — Cho toi them nuoc.
  • This is delicious! — Mon nay ngon qua!
  • Excuse me, this isn't what I ordered. — Xin loi, day khong phai mon toi goi.
  • Can I have the bill/check, please? — Tinh tien giup toi.

Thanh toan

  • Can we split the bill? — Chia tien duoc khong?
  • I'll get this. / It's on me. — De toi tra.
  • Do you accept cards? — Nhan the khong?
  • Is the tip included? — Tien tip da tinh chua?

So sanh voi tieng Viet

Giong: Ca hai ngon ngu deu co cau truc goi mon, hoi gia, yeu cau phuc vu tuong tu.

Khac:

  • Tieng Anh dung "I'll have..." (tuong lai) de goi mon. Tieng Viet: "Cho toi..." (menh lenh nhe).
  • Tieng Anh co van hoa tip (15-20% o My). Viet Nam khong bat buoc tip.
  • Tieng Anh phan biet "bill" (Anh) va "check" (My) — cung nghia. Tieng Viet chi co "tinh tien".
  • "I'm full" = no. Khong noi "I'm enough" (sai ngu phap).
  • Tieng Anh noi "It's on me" (de toi tra). Tieng Viet: "De toi" hoac "Toi moi".

Dialogue mau

Tinh huong 1: Goi mon tai nha hang

Waiter: Good evening! Welcome. Table for two?

You: Yes, please. Could we sit by the window?

Waiter: Of course. Here are your menus. Can I get you something to drink?

You: I'll have a sparkling water, please.

Friend: And a glass of red wine for me.

Waiter: Are you ready to order, or do you need a few more minutes?

You: I think we're ready. What do you recommend?

Waiter: The grilled salmon is very popular today.

You: Sounds good. I'll have that. And can I get it without the mushrooms? I'm allergic.

Waiter: No problem at all. And for you?

Friend: I'd like the steak, medium rare, please.

Waiter: Excellent choices. I'll be right back with your drinks.

Tinh huong 2: Quan cafe / goi nhanh

Barista: Hi! What can I get for you?

You: Can I have a large iced latte, please?

Barista: Sure! Any milk preference? We have oat, almond, and regular.

You: Oat milk, please. And a blueberry muffin.

Barista: For here or to go?

You: To go, please.

Barista: That'll be $8.50.

You: Here you go. Keep the change.

Barista: Thanks! Your latte will be ready at the end of the counter.

Loi thuong gap cua nguoi Viet

  • Sai: "I want the chicken." → Dung: "I'll have the chicken." / "I'd like the chicken." (lich su hon "want")
  • Sai: "Give me water." → Dung: "Could I have some water, please?" (them "please" va dung cau hoi)
  • Sai: "I'm enough." (khi no) → Dung: "I'm full." hoac "No more, thanks."
  • Sai: "The food is very delicious." → Dung: "The food is delicious." (delicious da la muc do cao, khong can "very")
  • Sai: "Can you calculate?" (tinh tien) → Dung: "Can I have the bill/check, please?"
  • Sai: "I eat vegetable." (an chay) → Dung: "I'm vegetarian." hoac "I don't eat meat."

Meo giao tiep

  • "I'll have..." la cach goi mon pho bien va lich su nhat.
  • Luon noi "please" khi yeu cau — thieu "please" nghe bat lich.
  • Tip o My: 15-20% tong hoa don. O Anh/Uc: 10% hoac khong bat buoc.
  • "For here or to go?" — cau hoi ban se nghe o moi quan fast food/cafe.
  • Dietary needs: Bao truoc neu di ung hoac an kieng — "I'm vegetarian / I'm allergic to nuts."

Bai tap

1. Ban muon goi mon ga. Noi cau nao lich su?
a) I want chicken.
b) Give me chicken.
c) I'll have the chicken, please.

c) I'll have the chicken, please.

2. Sua loi: "I'm enough, thank you." (khi da no)

I'm full, thank you.

3. Dien vao cho trong: "Could I have the ___, please?" (tinh tien — o My)

check (o My) hoac bill (o Anh)

4. Ban bi di ung voi dau phong. Noi the nao?

I'm allergic to peanuts. / Does this contain peanuts?

5. Phuc vu hoi "For here or to go?" — ban muon mang di. Tra loi?

To go, please.

6. Ban muon hoi mon dac biet. Noi the nao?

What's today's special? / What do you recommend?

7. Sua loi: "The food is very delicious."

The food is delicious. (khong can "very" — delicious da la muc do cao)

8. Ban muon chia tien voi ban. Noi the nao?

Can we split the bill? / Let's go Dutch.

9. Ban muon goi steak chin vua. Noi the nao?

I'd like the steak, medium, please. (rare = tai, medium = vua, well-done = chin ky)

10. Viet hoi thoai ngan (4-6 cau): ban goi do uong tai quan cafe.

Vi du:
Barista: Hi! What can I get you?
You: Can I have a medium cappuccino, please?
Barista: Hot or iced?
You: Hot, please. And do you have any pastries?
Barista: We have croissants and banana bread today.
You: I'll take a croissant. How much is that?
Barista: That's $6.50 altogether.
