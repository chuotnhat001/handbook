Self Introduction

Gioi thieu ban than — ky nang giao tiep co ban nhat khi gap nguoi moi

Mau cau co ban

Greeting + Name + Origin + Job + Hobby/Interest

Hi, I'm [name]. I'm from [place].
I work as a [job] / I'm a [job].
In my free time, I like [hobby].

Greeting (Chao hoi)

  • Hi / Hello — thong dung, moi tinh huong
  • Hey — than mat, ban be
  • Good morning / afternoon / evening — trang trong hon
  • Nice to meet you — khi gap lan dau
  • Pleased to meet you — trang trong hon "nice to meet you"

Name (Ten)

  • I'm Minh. — ngan gon, pho bien
  • My name is Minh. — day du hon
  • You can call me Minh. — khi ten kho doc
  • What's your name? — hoi ten nguoi khac

Origin (Que quan)

  • I'm from Vietnam.
  • I'm Vietnamese.
  • I come from Ho Chi Minh City.
  • I was born in Da Nang but I live in Hanoi now.

Job (Nghe nghiep)

  • I'm a software engineer.
  • I work as a teacher.
  • I work at a tech company.
  • I'm currently looking for a job.
  • I'm a student at [university].

Hobby / Interest (So thich)

  • I like reading and coding.
  • I enjoy playing football.
  • I'm interested in AI and technology.
  • In my free time, I usually watch movies.

So sanh voi tieng Viet

Giong: Ca hai ngon ngu deu gioi thieu theo thu tu: ten → que → nghe → so thich.

Khac:

  • Tieng Anh dung "I'm" (I am) cho ca ten, nghe, quoc tich. Tieng Viet dung "Toi la / Toi ten la".
  • Tieng Anh phan biet "I'm a teacher" (co mao tu "a"). Tieng Viet khong can: "Toi la giao vien".
  • Tieng Anh noi "I'm from Vietnam" (gioi tu "from"). Tieng Viet: "Toi den tu Viet Nam" hoac "Toi la nguoi Viet Nam".
  • Khi noi so thich, tieng Anh dung V-ing sau "like/enjoy": "I like reading". Tieng Viet khong chia dong tu.

Dialogue mau

Tinh huong 1: Gap dong nghiep moi

A: Hi! I'm Minh. I just joined the team this week.

B: Hey Minh, nice to meet you! I'm Sarah. How are you finding it so far?

A: It's great! Everyone is really friendly. I'm a backend developer — I'll be working on the API team.

B: Oh cool, I'm on the frontend side. Where are you from originally?

A: I'm from Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I moved here about two years ago.

B: Nice! Well, welcome to the team. Let me know if you need anything.

A: Thanks, Sarah. I appreciate it!

Tinh huong 2: Networking event

A: Good evening! I don't think we've met. I'm Lan.

B: Pleased to meet you, Lan. I'm David. What do you do?

A: I work as a product manager at a fintech startup. How about you?

B: I'm a UX designer. I've been freelancing for about three years now.

A: That sounds interesting! What kind of projects do you usually work on?

B: Mostly mobile apps. I'm really interested in accessibility design.

A: That's a growing field. Here's my card — let's keep in touch!

Loi thuong gap cua nguoi Viet

  • Sai: "I am Minh, 25 years old." → Dung: "I'm Minh. I'm 25 years old." (tach thanh 2 cau)
  • Sai: "My hobby is play guitar." → Dung: "My hobby is playing guitar." (V-ing sau "is")
  • Sai: "I'm engineer." → Dung: "I'm an engineer." (can mao tu "a/an")
  • Sai: "I come from Vietnamese." → Dung: "I come from Vietnam." (dung ten nuoc, khong dung tinh tu)
  • Sai: "I interesting in music." → Dung: "I'm interested in music." (dung tinh tu, khong dung dong tu)
  • Sai: "Nice to meet you" khi gap lai → Dung: "Nice to see you again." (meet = lan dau, see = gap lai)

Meo giao tiep

  • KISS — Keep It Short and Simple. Khong can noi qua nhieu thong tin cung luc.
  • Hoi lai — Sau khi gioi thieu, hoi nguoi khac: "How about you?" / "What about you?"
  • Cuoi va giao tiep mat — Quan trong khong kem loi noi.
  • Chuan bi san — Viet san 3-4 cau gioi thieu va luyen noi truoc guong.

Bai tap

1. Dien vao cho trong: "Hi, I'm Lan. I ___ from Hanoi."

am / 'm (I'm from Hanoi.)

2. Sua loi: "My hobby is cook Vietnamese food."

My hobby is cooking Vietnamese food. (V-ing sau "is")

3. Dien vao cho trong: "I work ___ a software developer ___ a tech company."

I work as a software developer at a tech company.

4. Sua loi: "I interesting in artificial intelligence."

I'm interested in artificial intelligence.

5. Viet lai cho dung: "I am teacher."

I am a teacher. (can mao tu "a" truoc danh tu chi nghe nghiep)

6. Dien vao cho trong: "Nice to ___ you! I'm Minh." (gap lan dau)

Nice to meet you!

7. Sap xep lai: "from / I / Vietnam / come"

I come from Vietnam.

8. Sua loi: "I come from Vietnamese."

I come from Vietnam. (dung ten nuoc, khong dung tinh tu chi quoc tich)

9. Viet 4 cau gioi thieu ban than (ten, que, nghe, so thich).

Vi du: Hi, I'm Minh. I'm from Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I work as a software engineer. In my free time, I enjoy reading books and playing chess.

10. Ban gap lai dong nghiep cu. Chon cau dung:
a) Nice to meet you again.
b) Nice to see you again.

b) Nice to see you again. ("meet" chi dung khi gap lan dau)
