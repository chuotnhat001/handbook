Day 09: Past Continuous — Formation

Cấu trúc was/were + V-ing, dùng với at that time, at 8 PM last night

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: Past Continuous

Phần A: Chia động từ ở thì quá khứ tiếp diễn

1. She ___ (read) a book at 9 PM last night.

2. The kids ___ (play) outside at that time.

3. I ___ (cook) dinner when you called.

4. He ___ (sleep) at midnight.

5. They ___ (watch) TV all evening yesterday.

6. It ___ (rain) heavily at 7 AM this morning.

7. We ___ (study) in the library at that time.

8. She ___ (wait) for the bus when I saw her.

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng

9. At 10 PM last night, I ___ a movie.

10. They ___ dinner when the phone rang.

11. She ___ not working at 3 PM yesterday.

12. ___ you sleeping when I called?

13. He ___ listening to music at that moment.

14. The students ___ taking an exam at 2 PM.

15. What ___ she doing at 6 o'clock?

16. I ___ not driving at that time. I was at home.

Phần C: Sửa lỗi sai

17. "They was talking loudly." → Sửa lại:

18. "She were running in the park." → Sửa lại:

19. "I was siting on the bench." → Sửa lại:

20. "He was not sleep at that time." → Sửa lại:

Phần D: Viết câu hoàn chỉnh

21. (she / study / at 8 PM last night) → Viết câu:

22. (they / not work / yesterday afternoon) → Viết câu phủ định:

23. (he / drive / at that time?) → Viết câu hỏi:

24. (we / have / dinner / at 7 PM) → Viết câu:

25. (I / not watch / TV / at that moment) → Viết câu phủ định:
