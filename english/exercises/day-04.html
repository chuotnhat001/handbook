Day 04: Present Continuous — Formation & Usage

Cấu trúc am/is/are + V-ing, dùng với right now, at the moment

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: Present Continuous

Phần A: Chia động từ ở thì hiện tại tiếp diễn

1. She ___ (read) a book right now.

2. The children ___ (play) in the garden at the moment.

3. I ___ (study) for my exam right now.

4. He ___ (cook) dinner at the moment.

5. We ___ (watch) a movie right now.

6. The dog ___ (run) in the park.

7. She ___ (sit) on the sofa.

8. I ___ (write) an email to my teacher.

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng

9. Look! It ___ outside.

10. They ___ to music at the moment.

11. She ___ her homework now.

12. I ___ TV right now. I'm busy.

13. ___ he sleeping? — Yes, he is.

14. We ___ for the bus at the moment.

15. The baby ___ right now. Don't make noise.

16. ___ you coming to the party tonight?

Phần C: Sửa lỗi sai

17. "She talking on the phone." → Sửa lại:

18. "They is swimming in the pool." → Sửa lại:

19. "He is makeing a cake." → Sửa lại:

20. "I amn't working today." → Sửa lại:

Phần D: Viết câu hoàn chỉnh

21. (she / listen / to music / right now) → Viết câu:

22. (they / not study / at the moment) → Viết câu phủ định:

23. (he / play / football / now?) → Viết câu hỏi:

24. (we / have / lunch / at the moment) → Viết câu:

25. (I / not sleep) → Viết câu phủ định:
