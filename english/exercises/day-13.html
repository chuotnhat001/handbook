Day 13: Will vs Going to

Chọn đúng dạng tương lai dựa vào ngữ cảnh: quyết định tức thì vs kế hoạch, dự đoán chung vs bằng chứng

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: will vs going to

Phần A: Chọn will hoặc going to

1. "I'm hungry." — "Wait here. I ___ make you a sandwich." (spontaneous decision)

2. She has bought a ticket. She ___ fly to London next week. (plan)

3. I think Brazil ___ win the World Cup. (opinion/prediction)

4. Look at those clouds! It ___ rain. (evidence-based prediction)

5. "The phone is ringing!" — "I ___ get it." (spontaneous)

6. We've decided. We ___ paint the kitchen blue. (plan)

7. I promise I ___ never lie to you again. (promise)

8. He's carrying a suitcase. He ___ travel somewhere. (evidence)

Phần B: Điền will hoặc be going to + verb

9. "Would you like tea or coffee?" — "I ___ (have) coffee, please." (spontaneous)

10. She's studied very hard. She ___ (be) a great doctor. (evidence)

11. "I don't have enough money." — "Don't worry, I ___ (lend) you some." (offer)

12. We've booked the hotel. We ___ (visit) Paris in May. (plan)

13. I believe technology ___ (be) even more important in the future. (opinion)

14. That driver is going too fast! He ___ (crash)! (evidence)

15. I've enrolled in a course. I ___ (start) learning Spanish next month. (plan)

16. It's cold. I ___ (close) the window. (spontaneous decision)

Phần C: Sửa lỗi sai

17. "The doorbell is ringing." — "I am going to answer it." (should be spontaneous) → Sửa lại:

18. "She will have a baby." (She's 8 months pregnant — evidence) → Sửa lại:

19. "I am going to help you." (just decided now, offer) → Sửa lại:

20. "We will move to London." (already planned, signed contract) → Sửa lại:

Phần D: Viết câu hoàn chỉnh

21. (I think / it / be / hot / tomorrow) → Dự đoán chung:

22. (they / get married / in June) → Kế hoạch đã quyết định:

23. (I / carry / that / for you) → Đề nghị tức thì:

24. (he / fail / the exam) → Bằng chứng: anh ấy không học gì cả:

25. (I / always / remember / this day) → Lời hứa:
