Day 12: Future — Going to

Plans, intentions, and evidence-based predictions with be going to

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: Future — going to

Phần A: Điền vào chỗ trống (be going to + verb)

1. She ___ (travel) to Japan next summer. She already bought the ticket.

2. They ___ (move) to a new house next month.

3. I ___ (study) medicine at university. I've already applied.

4. Look at those black clouds! It ___ (rain).

5. Be careful! That glass ___ (fall) off the table!

6. We ___ (have) a party this weekend. Everything is planned.

7. He ___ (cook) dinner tonight. He bought all the ingredients.

8. I ___ (not/eat) fast food anymore. I want to be healthy.

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng

9. She's packed her bags. She ___ on holiday.

10. Look! That car is driving too fast. It ___ crash!

11. We've decided. We ___ buy a new car.

12. He's been studying all week. He ___ pass the test.

13. I ___ start a new job next Monday. I signed the contract.

14. They ___ get married in June. The invitations are sent.

15. The sky is clear. It ___ be a beautiful day.

16. What ___ you ___ do after graduation?
