Day 16: Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Phân biệt thì hiện tại hoàn thành và quá khứ đơn — dấu hiệu thời gian, thời gian kết thúc vs chưa kết thúc

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Phần A: Chọn thì đúng

1. I ___ to Japan twice. (experience, no specific time)

2. She ___ to Paris last summer. (specific time in the past)

3. They ___ here since 2018.

4. He ___ his job two weeks ago.

5. I ___ never ___ such a beautiful sunset.

6. We ___ a great time at the party yesterday.

7. She ___ three cups of coffee today.

8. Mozart ___ more than 600 pieces of music.

Phần B: Điền đúng dạng động từ

9. ___ (you / ever / try) bungee jumping?

10. I ___ (buy) this phone last month.

11. She ___ (work) at this company for five years.

12. Where ___ (you / go) on holiday last year?

13. We ___ (not / finish) the project yet.

14. I ___ (meet) her at a conference in 2022.

15. The train ___ (just / arrive). Let's get on.

16. He ___ (graduate) from university in 2020.

Phần C: Sửa lỗi sai

17. "I lived here for ten years." (still living here) → Sửa lại:

18. "She has gone to London last week." → Sửa lại:

19. "I have seen that movie yesterday." → Sửa lại:

20. "He was a teacher since 2015." (still a teacher) → Sửa lại:

Phần D: Viết câu hoàn chỉnh

21. (I / know / him / for ten years) → Present Perfect (still know):

22. (she / visit / Rome / in 2019) → Past Simple:

23. (we / not / see / her / today) → Present Perfect:

24. (you / eat / breakfast / this morning?) → Past Simple (morning is over):

25. (they / already / leave) → Present Perfect:
