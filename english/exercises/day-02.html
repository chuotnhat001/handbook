Day 02: Present Simple — Frequency Adverbs & Negatives

Trạng từ tần suất (always/usually/never) và câu phủ định (don't/doesn't)

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: Frequency Adverbs & Negatives

Phần A: Điền trạng từ tần suất vào đúng vị trí

1. She ___ gets up at 6 AM. (100% of the time)

2. He ___ walks to work. (about 80% of the time)

3. They ___ eat fast food. (0% of the time)

4. We ___ go to the cinema on weekends. (about 70%)

5. I ___ read books before bed. (about 50%)

6. She ___ watches horror movies. (about 10%)

7. The bus is ___ late in the morning. (100%)

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng (don't / doesn't / trạng từ)

8. He ___ drink coffee in the evening.

9. They ___ like waking up early.

10. She ___ eats breakfast. She prefers to skip it.

11. My father ___ smoke. He quit years ago.

12. We ___ go swimming in winter. It's too cold.

13. He is ___ late for class. The teacher is angry.

14. She ___ understand the homework.

15. I ___ forget my keys. Maybe once a year.

16. The children ___ play outside when it rains.

Phần C: Sửa lỗi sai

17. "He don't like vegetables." → Sửa lại:

18. "She arrives always on time." → Sửa lại:

19. "They doesn't have a car." → Sửa lại:

20. "I don't never drink soda." → Sửa lại:

21. "She not work on Sundays." → Sửa lại:

Phần D: Viết câu hoàn chỉnh

22. (he / usually / play / tennis / on Saturdays) → Viết câu:

23. (she / not always / eat / lunch) → Viết câu phủ định:

24. (I / never / watch / TV / in the morning) → Viết câu:

25. (they / not often / go / to the gym) → Viết câu phủ định:
