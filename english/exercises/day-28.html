Day 28: Final Test 3 — Conditionals & Prepositions

Kiểm tra câu điều kiện (Type 1 & 2) và giới từ (time & place)

30 câu ~45 phút Chủ đề: Conditionals & Prepositions

Phần A: Conditionals — Chọn đáp án đúng (câu 1-10)

1. If it rains tomorrow, we ___ stay home.

2. If I ___ you, I would accept the offer.

3. She will pass the exam if she ___ hard.

4. If he had more money, he ___ buy a new car.

5. If you heat water to 100°C, it ___.

6. If I won the lottery, I ___ travel the world.

7. If you ___ early, you won't miss the bus.

8. What ___ you do if you found a wallet on the street?

9. If she ___ sick, she will go to the doctor.

10. If they lived near the sea, they ___ go swimming every day.

Phần B: Prepositions — Điền in/on/at (câu 11-22)

11. The meeting starts ___ 9 AM.

12. She was born ___ December.

13. We have a test ___ Friday.

14. He lives ___ a big city.

15. The painting is ___ the wall.

16. I'll meet you ___ the entrance.

17. They arrived ___ the morning.

18. Her birthday is ___ March 15th.

19. He is ___ home right now.

20. The keys are ___ my pocket.

21. She is sitting ___ the sofa.

22. We eat lunch ___ noon.
