Day 27: Final Test 2 — Modals & Comparatives

Kiểm tra động từ khuyết thiếu (can, must, should, may, might, have to) và so sánh

30 câu ~45 phút Chủ đề: Modals & Comparatives

Phần A: Modals — Chọn đáp án đúng (câu 1-15)

1. You ___ drive without a license. It's illegal.

2. She ___ speak three languages fluently.

3. You look tired. You ___ go to bed early tonight.

4. It ___ rain later. Take an umbrella just in case.

5. Students ___ wear uniforms at this school. It's a rule.

6. You ___ finish this today. Tomorrow is fine too.

7. ___ I borrow your pen, please?

8. He ___ be at home. His car is in the driveway.

9. When I was young, I ___ run very fast.

10. You ___ eat more vegetables. It's good for your health.

11. She ___ swim when she was 4 years old.

12. You ___ smoke in the hospital. It's forbidden.

13. ___ you help me carry this box?

14. He ___ be the new teacher. He looks very young.

15. We ___ to hurry or we'll miss the train.

Phần B: Comparatives & Superlatives (câu 16-25)

16. He is ___ (tall) than his brother.

17. This is ___ (expensive) restaurant in the city.

18. Her English is ___ (good) than mine.

19. This temple is ___ (old) in Vietnam.

20. This book is ___ (interesting) than that one.

21. She is ___ student in the class.

22. A car is ___ than a bicycle.

23. This exercise is ___ than the last one.

24. Mount Everest is ___ mountain in the world.

25. His results are ___ than last semester.

Phần C: Viết câu (câu 26-30)

26. (you / must not / use phone / in class) → Viết câu cấm:

27. (she / can / play the piano / very well) → Viết câu khả năng:

28. (Tokyo / big / Hanoi) → Viết câu so sánh hơn:

29. (English / popular / language / in the world) → Viết câu so sánh nhất:

30. (you / should / drink more water / every day) → Viết câu khuyên:
