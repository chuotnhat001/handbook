Day 24: Conjunctions

Liên từ: and, but, or, so, because, although, when, if

25 câu ~35 phút Chủ đề: Conjunctions

Phần A: Điền liên từ phù hợp

1. She stayed home ___ she was sick.

2. He is rich ___ he is not happy.

3. It was raining, ___ we took an umbrella.

4. She likes reading ___ writing.

5. Would you like tea ___ coffee?

6. ___ it was cold, she went swimming.

7. I was sleeping ___ the phone rang.

8. I will help you ___ you ask me.

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng

9. I wanted to go out, ___ it was too late.

10. He studied hard ___ he wanted to pass the exam.

11. She was tired, ___ she continued working.

12. ___ he was late, the teacher didn't let him in.

13. You can have the red one ___ the blue one.

14. ___ she is young, she is very talented.

15. ___ I finish my homework, I will watch TV.

16. ___ you study hard, you will fail the test.
