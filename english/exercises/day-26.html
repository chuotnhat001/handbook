Day 26: Final Test 1 — Tenses

Bài kiểm tra tổng hợp tất cả các thì đã học (Present, Past, Future, Perfect)

30 câu ~45 phút Chủ đề: All Tenses

Phần A: Chọn thì đúng (câu 1-15)

1. She ___ to work every day by bus.

2. Right now, they ___ a new project.

3. He ___ his homework before dinner yesterday.

4. We ___ in this house since 2015.

5. I ___ you tomorrow at 3 PM.

6. While I ___ home, I saw an accident.

7. By the time you arrive, I ___ cooking.

8. She ___ never ___ sushi before last week.

9. The children ___ in the park right now.

10. He ___ three books last month.

11. I ___ for two hours. I need a break.

12. This time next week, we ___ on the beach.

13. Water ___ at 100 degrees Celsius.

14. They ___ married for 20 years next June.

15. She ___ when the doorbell rang.

Phần B: Chia động từ đúng thì (câu 16-25)

16. Mr. Tran ___ (teach) English at our school.

17. The students ___ (wait) for the bus now.

18. She ___ (buy) a new phone last week.

19. I ___ (know) him for ten years.

20. She ___ (be) 25 years old next month.

21. He ___ (read) a book when I entered the room.

22. By the time we arrived, the train ___ (leave).

23. It ___ (rain) since this morning.

24. She ___ (not/eat) meat. She is vegetarian.

25. By 2027, they ___ (complete) the new bridge.

Phần C: Viết câu hoàn chỉnh (câu 26-30)

27. (they / play tennis / when / it / start raining) → Past Continuous + Past Simple:

28. (I / travel to Japan / next Monday) → Future Continuous:

29. (he / already eat / before / she / arrive) → Past Perfect + Past Simple:

30. (we / not / watch TV / every night) → Present Simple negative:
