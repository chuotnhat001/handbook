Day 15: Present Perfect — since/for/already/yet/just/ever/never

Sử dụng các trạng từ và giới từ thời gian với thì hiện tại hoàn thành

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: Present Perfect — Time markers

Phần A: Điền since hoặc for

1. I have lived here ___ ten years.

2. She has worked here ___ 2019.

3. We have been friends ___ a long time.

4. He has been sick ___ Monday.

5. They have studied English ___ three months.

6. I haven't seen her ___ last summer.

7. He has played guitar ___ five years.

8. We have known each other ___ childhood.

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng (already/yet/just/ever/never)

9. Have you ___ been to Australia?

10. I have ___ finished my homework. I did it an hour ago.

11. She has ___ arrived. She walked in 2 minutes ago.

12. Has he called you ___?

13. I have ___ eaten snails. I don't want to try.

14. They haven't replied to my email ___.

15. We have ___ seen this movie. Let's watch something else.

16. Have you ___ tried Vietnamese food?

Phần C: Sửa lỗi sai

17. "I have lived here for 2010." → Sửa lại:

18. "She has left already." → Sửa lại (đặt already đúng vị trí):

19. "He has ever been to Paris." (meaning: chưa bao giờ) → Sửa lại:

20. "They have known each other since years." → Sửa lại:

Phần D: Viết câu hoàn chỉnh

21. (I / just / finish / my dinner) → Viết câu:

22. (she / not / eat / yet) → Viết câu phủ định:

23. (you / ever / see / a whale?) → Viết câu hỏi:

24. (we / live / here / since 2015) → Viết câu:

25. (he / already / do / his homework) → Viết câu:
