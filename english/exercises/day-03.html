Day 03: Present Simple — Questions & Short Answers

Phần A: Điền Do hoặc Does

1. ___ she like chocolate?

2. ___ you play any sports?

3. ___ he work on weekends?

4. ___ they live near the school?

5. ___ it rain a lot in your city?

6. ___ we need to bring anything?
