Day 21: Conditionals Type 2

If + past simple → would + V (tình huống không có thật ở hiện tại, giả định)

25 câu ~35 phút Chủ đề: Conditionals Type 2

Phần A: Điền động từ đúng dạng (If clause)

1. If I had a lot of money, I ___ (buy) a big house.

2. If she won the lottery, she ___ (travel) around the world.

3. If I were you, I ___ (help) him immediately.

4. If he ___ (live) near the beach, he would swim every day.

5. If I spoke French, it ___ (be) easier to live in Paris.

6. If she ___ (know) the answer, she would tell us.

7. If it rained tomorrow, we ___ (not/go) to the park.

8. If they ___ (have) more time, they would visit the museum.

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng

9. If I ___ rich, I would donate to charity.

10. If she had a car, she ___ drive to work.

11. If he studied harder, he ___ pass the exam.

12. What would you do if you ___ the president?

13. If I ___ you, I would apologize.

14. She would be happy if she ___ a better job.

15. If we lived in Japan, we ___ eat sushi every day.

16. If they ___ English well, they would get the job.
