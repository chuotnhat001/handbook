Day 23: Prepositions of Place

Sử dụng in / on / at cho các vị trí và địa điểm

25 câu ~35 phút Chủ đề: Prepositions of Place

Phần A: Điền in / on / at

1. She lives ___ a small apartment.

2. The book is ___ the table.

3. I'll meet you ___ the bus stop.

4. He works ___ Ho Chi Minh City.

5. There is a picture ___ the wall.

6. She is waiting ___ the door.

7. The children are playing ___ the garden.

8. My office is ___ the second floor.

9. We arrived ___ the airport early.

10. There are many fish ___ the river.

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng

11. The cat is sitting ___ the roof.

12. He is standing ___ the corner of the street.

13. She is ___ the kitchen cooking dinner.

14. I'll see you ___ school tomorrow.

15. Don't sit ___ the floor, it's dirty.

16. They live ___ Vietnam.

17. She works ___ home on Fridays.

18. I saw her ___ the bus this morning.

Phần C: Sửa lỗi sai

19. "She is in the office." (nghĩa: tại nơi làm việc, không nhấn mạnh bên trong) → Sửa lại:

20. "The keys are on my bag." (nghĩa: bên trong túi) → Sửa lại:

21. "He lives in the third floor." → Sửa lại:

Phần D: Viết câu hoàn chỉnh

22. (the cat / the chair — bề mặt) → Viết câu với giới từ đúng:

24. (many people / the park — bên trong khu vực) → Viết câu:
