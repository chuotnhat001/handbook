Day 14: Present Perfect — Formation

Cấu trúc have/has + V3 (past participle) — động từ có quy tắc và bất quy tắc

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: Present Perfect Formation

Phần A: Điền have/has + past participle

1. She ___ (visit) Paris three times.

2. We ___ (finish) our homework.

3. He ___ (eat) all the cake.

4. They ___ (live) here for ten years.

5. She ___ (write) five books.

6. I ___ (see) that movie before.

7. He ___ (go) to the supermarket.

8. They ___ (break) the window.

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng

9. She ___ never ___ sushi before.

10. I ___ already ___ my lunch.

11. They ___ to London many times.

12. He ___ his keys. He can't find them.

13. We ___ not ___ that book yet.

14. The past participle of "take" is ___.

15. The past participle of "do" is ___.

16. The past participle of "play" is ___.
