Day 30: Final Test 5 — Comprehensive

Bài kiểm tra tổng hợp cuối cùng — tất cả chủ đề từ Day 01 đến Day 25

30 câu ~45 phút Chủ đề: Comprehensive (All Topics)

Phần A: Tenses (câu 1-8)

1. She ___ English every day after school.

2. Look! The baby ___ .

3. They ___ to Da Lat last summer.

4. He ___ here since 2019.

5. This time tomorrow, she ___ on the plane to Seoul.

6. He ___ (watch) TV when the power went out.

7. By next June, she ___ (graduate) from university.

8. When we arrived at the cinema, the movie ___ (already/start).

Phần B: Modals (câu 9-14)

9. You ___ wear a helmet when riding a motorbike. It's the law.

10. She ___ play the guitar when she was 8.

11. You ___ eat so much sugar. It's bad for your teeth.

12. It ___ snow tonight. The sky looks very dark.

13. Tomorrow is Sunday. You ___ wake up early.

14. ___ I open the window? It's hot in here.

Phần C: Comparatives & Conditionals (câu 15-22)

15. This phone is ___ (expensive) than that one.

16. The cheetah is ___ (fast) animal on land.

17. Her cooking is ___ (good) than mine.

18. If you don't hurry, you ___ miss the train.

19. If I ___ a bird, I would fly to the mountains.

20. If she ___ the bus, she will be late for work.

21. (I / have a million dollars → start a business) → Conditional Type 2:

22. (you / eat too much → get sick) → Conditional Type 1:

Phần D: Prepositions & Conjunctions (câu 23-30)

23. The concert starts ___ 7:30 PM.

24. I have a dentist appointment ___ Thursday.

25. She has lived ___ London for five years.

26. The cat is sleeping ___ the bed.

27. She was hungry ___ she hadn't eaten all day.

28. He studied hard, ___ he failed the exam.

29. It was cold, ___ we turned on the heater.

30. ___ she was tired, she finished all her homework.
