Day 08: Past Simple — Negatives & Questions

Câu phủ định (didn't + V) và câu hỏi (Did + S + V?) ở quá khứ đơn

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: Past Simple — Negatives & Questions

Phần A: Viết câu phủ định với didn't

1. She ___ (not go) to the party last night.

2. I ___ (not eat) breakfast this morning.

3. They ___ (not watch) the movie yesterday.

4. He ___ (not buy) a new car.

5. We ___ (not sleep) well last night.

6. It ___ (not rain) yesterday.

7. She ___ (not finish) her homework.

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng

8. ___ you see the accident yesterday?

9. She didn't ___ to the meeting.

10. Did he ___ the exam?

11. They ___ enjoy the concert last week.

12. Did she ___ you the truth?

13. Where ___ you go last summer?

14. I didn't ___ anything wrong.

15. ___ they arrive on time? — No, they didn't.

16. What ___ she say to you?
