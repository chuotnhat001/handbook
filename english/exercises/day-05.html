Day 05: Present Simple vs Present Continuous

Phân biệt thì hiện tại đơn và hiện tại tiếp diễn dựa vào ngữ cảnh

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: Present Simple vs Continuous

Phần A: Chọn thì đúng

1. She ___ to work every day. (habit)

2. Look! The baby ___ .

3. Water ___ at 100 degrees Celsius. (fact)

4. Be quiet! I ___ to concentrate.

5. He usually ___ coffee for breakfast.

6. Right now, they ___ a new house.

7. The Earth ___ around the Sun. (fact)

8. She ___ on the phone at the moment.

Phần B: Điền dạng đúng của động từ

9. Take an umbrella. It ___ (rain) outside right now.

10. My father ___ (work) in a bank. He's been there for 10 years.

11. What ___ (you / do) right now? — I'm reading.

12. I ___ (not like) horror movies. They scare me.

13. Look at Tom! He ___ (wear) a funny hat today.

14. The movie ___ (start) at 8 PM every Friday.

15. I ___ (learn) to drive this month.

16. She ___ (speak) three languages fluently.

Phần C: Sửa lỗi sai

17. "I work now. Don't disturb me." → Sửa lại:

18. "She is going to school every day." → Sửa lại:

19. "He sleeps right now." → Sửa lại:

20. "I am liking chocolate." → Sửa lại (like = stative verb):

Phần D: Viết câu hoàn chỉnh

21. (she / usually / walk / to work) — habit → Viết câu:

22. (he / read / a book / now) — happening now → Viết câu:

23. (they / play / tennis / every Sunday) — routine → Viết câu:

24. (we / not watch / TV / right now) — happening now → Viết câu:

25. (the shop / close / at 9 PM) — schedule/fact → Viết câu:
