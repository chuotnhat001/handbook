Day 11: Future — Will

Predictions, spontaneous decisions, promises with will/won't

25 cau ~30 phut Chu de: Future — will

Phan A: Dien vao cho trong (will + verb)

1. Look at those dark clouds. It ___ (rain) soon.

2. Don't worry. I ___ (help) you with your homework.

3. I promise I ___ (call) you tomorrow.

4. She ___ (be) 20 years old next month.

5. I think he ___ (pass) the exam.

6. It's hot in here. I ___ (open) the window.

7. That bag looks heavy. I ___ (carry) it for you.

8. I promise I ___ (not/tell) anyone your secret.

Phan B: Chon dap an dung

9. "The phone is ringing." — "I ___ answer it."

10. I think robots ___ do most jobs in the future.

11. She ___ come to the party. She's too busy.

12. "We don't have any milk." — "I ___ go and buy some."

13. I believe the world ___ be a better place one day.

14. Don't touch that! You ___ burn yourself!

15. I ___ lend you my book. I don't need it today.

16. People ___ live on Mars in 100 years, I think.

Phan C: Sua loi sai

17. "I will helping you." → Sua lai:

18. "She will not comes." → Sua lai:

19. "Will you are there?" → Sua lai:

20. "It will is sunny tomorrow." → Sua lai:

Phan D: Viet cau hoan chinh

21. (I / always / love / you) → Viet cau voi "will" (promise):

22. (he / not / forget / his keys) → Viet cau phu dinh voi "will":

23. (they / arrive / on time?) → Viet cau hoi voi "will":

24. (I think / it / snow / tonight) → Viet cau du doan:

25. (she / be / a great doctor) → Viet cau du doan voi "will":
