Day 17: Modal Verbs — can/could/be able to

Khả năng, sự cho phép, và yêu cầu lịch sự với can, could, be able to

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: can/could/be able to

Phần A: Điền can, could, hoặc be able to

1. She ___ speak three languages fluently. (present ability)

2. When I was young, I ___ run very fast. (past ability)

3. ___ you help me with this box? (request)

4. ___ you please open the window? (polite request)

5. You ___ use my computer if you need to. (permission)

6. He ___ already read when he was four years old. (past ability)

7. I ___ see the mountains from my window. (present ability)

8. ___ I borrow your pen, please? (polite permission)

Phần B: Chọn đáp án đúng

9. She will ___ finish the project by Friday.

10. I ___ swim since I was six.

11. ___ you ride a bike when you were five?

12. You ___ park here. It's not allowed.

13. After months of practice, he was finally ___ play the song.

14. ___ you tell me the time, please?

15. I ___ hear you. Please speak louder.

16. When she was a child, she ___ play piano beautifully.
