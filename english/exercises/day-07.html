Day 07: Past Simple — Irregular Verbs

Động từ bất quy tắc ở quá khứ đơn (go→went, see→saw, buy→bought...)

25 câu ~30 phút Chủ đề: Past Simple — Irregular Verbs

Phần A: Chia động từ bất quy tắc

1. She ___ (go) to the market yesterday.

2. I ___ (see) a beautiful bird in the park.

3. He ___ (buy) a new phone last week.

4. We ___ (eat) sushi for dinner last night.

5. She ___ (take) the bus to school.

6. My mother ___ (make) a cake for my birthday.

7. He ___ (write) a letter to his friend.

8. They ___ (come) home very late.

14. She ___ a new dress for the wedding.

15. I ___ about you last night.

16. The children ___ in the lake yesterday.
