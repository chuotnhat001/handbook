Technology & Internet

Cong nghe va Internet — tu vung tech, mo ta van de ky thuat, va giao tiep trong moi truong so

Mau cau co ban

Mo ta van de ky thuat

My [device] isn't working. — [Thiet bi] cua toi khong hoat dong.
The app keeps crashing. — Ung dung cu bi crash.
I can't connect to the Wi-Fi. — Toi khong ket noi duoc Wi-Fi.
The page won't load. — Trang khong tai duoc.
Have you tried restarting it? — Ban thu khoi dong lai chua?

Hoi ve cong nghe

  • What's your Wi-Fi password? — Mat khau Wi-Fi la gi?
  • How do I set this up? — Cai dat cai nay the nao?
  • Is there an app for that? — Co app nao cho viec do khong?
  • Can you send me the link? — Gui link cho toi duoc khong?
  • What platform do you use for [X]? — Ban dung nen tang nao cho [X]?

Mang xa hoi & Online

  • I'll send you a friend request. — Toi se gui loi moi ket ban.
  • Did you see my post? — Ban thay bai dang cua toi chua?
  • I'll DM you. — Toi se nhan tin rieng cho ban.
  • Can you share your screen? — Ban chia se man hinh duoc khong?
  • You're on mute. — Ban dang bi tat mic.
  • The connection is laggy. — Ket noi bi lag.

So sanh voi tieng Viet

Giong: Ca hai ngon ngu deu muon nhieu tu vung tech tu tieng Anh (app, Wi-Fi, laptop, online, stream...).

Khac:

  • Tieng Viet muon truc tiep nhieu tu tech (download, update, bug, crash). Tieng Anh la nguon goc cua cac tu nay.
  • Tieng Anh dung phrasal verbs nhieu trong tech: "log in", "sign up", "set up", "back up". Tieng Viet thuong dung tu muon: "dang nhap", "dang ky".
  • Tieng Anh phan biet "turn on" (bat) va "turn off" (tat) vs "restart" (khoi dong lai). Tieng Viet tuong tu nhung khong co phrasal verb.
  • "It won't load" dung "won't" (tu choi) — nhu the may tinh "khong chiu" lam. Tieng Viet: "khong tai duoc".

Dialogue mau

Tinh huong 1: Xu ly van de ky thuat

You: Hey, my laptop keeps freezing. It's been happening all morning.

Friend: Have you tried restarting it?

You: Yeah, twice. It works fine for about 10 minutes, then freezes again.

Friend: How much storage do you have left? If your disk is almost full, that could cause it.

You: Let me check... Oh, I only have 2 GB left. That might be the problem.

Friend: Yeah, try deleting some old files or moving them to the cloud. Also, check if there are any apps running in the background that you don't need.

You: Good idea. I'll clean up some stuff and see if that helps.

Tinh huong 2: Hop online

Host: Can everyone hear me? Minh, I think you're on mute.

Minh: Oh sorry, can you hear me now?

Host: Yes, perfect. Can you share your screen? I'd like to see the demo.

Minh: Sure, one second... Can you see my screen?

Host: Yes, but it's a bit blurry. The connection seems laggy on your end.

Minh: Let me turn off my camera to save bandwidth. Is that better?

Host: Much better, thanks. Go ahead with the demo.

Loi thuong gap cua nguoi Viet

  • Sai: "Open the internet." → Dung: "Open the browser." hoac "Go online." (internet la mang, browser la trinh duyet)
  • Sai: "I download the app yesterday." → Dung: "I downloaded the app yesterday." (qua khu → V-ed)
  • Sai: "Please give me the wifi." → Dung: "What's the Wi-Fi password?" (hoi mat khau, khong phai "cho wifi")
  • Sai: "My phone is no battery." → Dung: "My phone is dead." hoac "My phone's out of battery."
  • Sai: "I will inbox you." → Dung: "I'll DM you." hoac "I'll message you." (inbox khong dung lam dong tu trong tieng Anh chuan)
  • Sai: "The wifi is slow speed." → Dung: "The Wi-Fi is slow." hoac "The connection is laggy."

Meo giao tiep

  • "Have you tried restarting it?" — cau hoi dau tien khi ai do gap van de tech. Thuong giai quyet 50% van de.
  • Phrasal verbs rat pho bien trong tech — hoc chung theo cap: log in/out, sign up/in, turn on/off, set up/back up.
  • "You're on mute" — cau ban se nghe (va noi) nhieu nhat trong hop online.
  • Dung "it won't [verb]" khi thiet bi tu choi lam gi do: "It won't turn on", "It won't connect".
  • Tech support: Mo ta van de ro rang — "what happened, when, what you tried" — giup nguoi khac ho tro nhanh hon.

Bai tap

1. Sua loi: "I download the update yesterday but it not work."

I downloaded the update yesterday but it didn't work.

2. Dien phrasal verb: "Don't forget to ___ ___ your files before updating the OS."

back up your files

3. Sua loi: "Please give me the wifi."

What's the Wi-Fi password?

4. Dien thi het pin dien thoai: "Sorry, I can't call you — my phone is ___."

my phone is dead (hoac "out of battery")

5. Trong hop online, ban khong nghe duoc dong nghiep. Noi the nao?

I think you're on mute. / I can't hear you — could you check your mic?

6. Phan biet "sign up" va "sign in":
a) Tao tai khoan moi = ___
b) Dang nhap tai khoan da co = ___

a) sign up
b) sign in (hoac log in)

7. Sua loi: "I will inbox you the details."

I'll DM you the details. / I'll send you the details.

8. Laptop khong chiu bat. Mo ta van de (2 cau).

My laptop won't turn on. I've tried plugging it in and holding the power button, but nothing happens.

9. Dien vao cho trong: "Can you ___ your screen? I'd like to see the code."

share your screen

10. Viet 3-4 cau mo ta mot van de tech ban gap gan day (dung Past Simple + phrasal verbs).

Vi du: Yesterday my phone ran out of storage, so I couldn't download any new apps. I backed up my photos to Google Drive and deleted the originals. After that, I freed up about 5 GB and everything worked fine again.
