Daily Routine

Thoi quen hang ngay — mo ta nhung viec ban lam moi ngay bang tieng Anh

Ngu phap trong tam

Present Simple dung de mo ta thoi quen hang ngay:

S + V(s/es) + time expression
I wake up at 6 a.m. every day.
She goes to work by bus.

Adverbs of frequency: always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never
Vi tri: truoc dong tu thuong, sau "be"
I usually have breakfast at 7. / She is always on time.

Time expressions (Cum tu chi thoi gian)

  • at + gio cu the: at 6 a.m., at noon, at midnight
  • in + buoi: in the morning, in the afternoon, in the evening
  • at + night (ngoai le: khong dung "in")
  • every + day/morning/week: every morning, every weekend
  • on + thu: on Monday, on weekdays

Phrasal verbs thuong gap

  • wake up — thuc day
  • get up — roi giuong
  • get dressed — mac quan ao
  • go to work/school — di lam/di hoc
  • get home — ve den nha
  • work out / exercise — tap the duc
  • hang out — di choi voi ban
  • stay up late — thuc khuya
  • go to bed — di ngu

So sanh voi tieng Viet

Giong: Ca hai ngon ngu deu mo ta thoi quen theo trinh tu thoi gian (sang → trua → toi).

Khac:

  • Tieng Anh chia dong tu theo ngo thu 3 so it (he/she/it + V-s/es). Tieng Viet khong chia.
  • Tieng Anh dung gioi tu truoc thoi gian (at 6, in the morning). Tieng Viet dat thoi gian linh hoat hon.
  • Tieng Anh co adverbs of frequency voi vi tri co dinh. Tieng Viet dung "thuong", "luon" linh hoat hon.
  • Tieng Anh phan biet "wake up" (thuc) va "get up" (roi giuong). Tieng Viet thuong gop chung "day".

Dialogue mau

Tinh huong 1: Hoi ve thoi quen hang ngay

A: What does your typical day look like?

B: Well, I usually wake up around 6:30. I make coffee, check my emails, and then start working by 8.

A: Do you work from home?

B: Yes, most days. I take a lunch break at noon — usually just a quick sandwich. Then I work until about 5:30.

A: What do you do after work?

B: I go for a run or hit the gym. After dinner, I usually read or watch something on Netflix. I try to go to bed before 11.

Tinh huong 2: So sanh thoi quen

A: Are you a morning person or a night owl?

B: Definitely a night owl. I rarely go to bed before midnight. How about you?

A: I'm the opposite! I wake up at 5 a.m. every day — even on weekends.

B: Wow, that's early! What do you do that early?

A: I meditate for 20 minutes, then go for a walk. It helps me feel more focused during the day.

B: I should try that. My mornings are always rushed — I usually hit snooze three times before getting up.

Loi thuong gap cua nguoi Viet

  • Sai: "I wake up at 6 hour." → Dung: "I wake up at 6 o'clock / at 6 a.m."
  • Sai: "I go to work by walk." → Dung: "I walk to work." hoac "I go to work on foot."
  • Sai: "She go to school every day." → Dung: "She goes to school every day." (ngo thu 3 + s)
  • Sai: "I usually am tired." → Dung: "I am usually tired." (adverb sau "be")
  • Sai: "In night I watch TV." → Dung: "At night I watch TV." (dung "at" voi night)
  • Sai: "I sleep at 11 p.m." → Dung: "I go to bed at 11 p.m." (sleep = dang ngu, go to bed = hanh dong di ngu)

Meo giao tiep

  • Dung sequencing words de ke chuyen tron tru: First, Then, After that, Finally.
  • Hoi lai de tao hoi thoai: "How about you?" / "What about your morning?"
  • Them chi tiet de tu nhien hon: thay vi "I eat breakfast", noi "I usually grab a quick coffee and some toast."
  • Morning person vs Night owl — cach hoi thu vi ve thoi quen ngu.

Bai tap

1. Dien vao cho trong: "She always ___ (get up) at 7 a.m."

gets up (ngo thu 3 so it + s)

2. Chon gioi tu dung: "I have lunch ___ noon." (at / in / on)

at noon

3. Sap xep lai: "usually / I / coffee / drink / the morning / in"

I usually drink coffee in the morning.

4. Sua loi: "He go to work by bus every day."

He goes to work by bus every day.

5. Dien adverb of frequency: "I ___ eat fast food. Maybe once a month." (always / usually / rarely)

rarely (chi 1 lan/thang = rat it khi)

6. Sua loi: "In night, I usually watch YouTube."

At night, I usually watch YouTube.

7. Phan biet: "wake up" va "get up" — dien vao cho trong:
"My alarm rings at 6 but I don't ___ until 6:30."

get up (wake up = mo mat, get up = roi giuong)

8. Viet lai cho tu nhien hon: "I eat breakfast at 7. I go to work at 8. I come home at 6."

I usually have breakfast around 7, then head to work by 8. I get home at about 6.

9. Dien vao cho trong: "Are you a morning ___ or a night ___?"

morning person / night owl

10. Mo ta thoi quen buoi sang cua ban bang 4-5 cau (dung Present Simple + time expressions).

Vi du: I wake up at 6:30 every morning. First, I brush my teeth and take a quick shower. Then I make coffee and check my phone. I usually leave for work around 7:45. I always listen to a podcast on my commute.
