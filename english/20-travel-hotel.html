Travel & Hotel

Du lich va khach san — dat phong, check-in, san bay, va cac tinh huong khi di du lich

Tai san bay

Check-in va boarding

I'd like to check in for my flight to [destination].
Here's my passport and booking confirmation.
Can I have a window/aisle seat?
How many bags can I check in?
What gate is my flight?
Is the flight on time?

Cac tinh huong o san bay

  • Where is the departure gate? — Cong khoi hanh o dau?
  • My flight has been delayed/cancelled. — Chuyen bay bi tre/huy.
  • I missed my connecting flight. — Toi lo chuyen bay noi chuyen.
  • Where can I pick up my luggage? — Lay hanh ly o dau?
  • I'd like to declare something. — Toi muon khai bao (hai quan).
  • Nothing to declare. — Khong co gi khai bao.

Tai khach san

Dat phong

  • I'd like to book a room for [number] nights. — Toi muon dat phong [so] dem.
  • Do you have any vacancies? — Con phong trong khong?
  • How much is it per night? — Bao nhieu mot dem?
  • Is breakfast included? — Co bao gom bua sang khong?
  • I'd like a single/double room. — Toi muon phong don/doi.
  • Do you have a room with a view? — Co phong co view khong?

Check-in / Check-out

I have a reservation under [name].
What time is check-out?
Can I have a late check-out? — Tra phong muon duoc khong?
Could I leave my luggage here after check-out?
Is there Wi-Fi? What's the password?

Yeu cau / Van de

  • Could I get extra towels? — Cho toi them khan duoc khong?
  • The air conditioning isn't working. — May lanh khong hoat dong.
  • I'd like to change rooms. — Toi muon doi phong.
  • Is there a gym/pool in the hotel? — Khach san co phong gym/ho boi khong?
  • Can you call a taxi for me? — Goi taxi giup toi duoc khong?

So sanh voi tieng Viet

Giong: Ca hai ngon ngu deu co cac mau cau dat phong, hoi gia, yeu cau dich vu tuong tu.

Khac:

  • Tieng Anh dung "I'd like to..." (lich su) thay vi "I want" khi dat phong/yeu cau. Tieng Viet: "Toi muon..." dung cho ca hai.
  • Tieng Anh phan biet "book" (dat truoc) va "check in" (lam thu tuc). Tieng Viet thuong dung "dat phong" cho ca hai.
  • Tieng Anh noi "per night" (moi dem). Tieng Viet: "mot dem" — tuong tu nhung khong co "per".
  • "Vacancies" = phong trong (thuong thay tren bien khach san). Tieng Viet: "con phong khong?"
  • Tieng Anh dung "under [name]" khi noi dat phong duoi ten ai. Tieng Viet: "duoi ten [ten]".

Dialogue mau

Tinh huong 1: Check-in khach san

Receptionist: Good afternoon! Welcome to the Grand Hotel. How can I help you?

You: Hi, I have a reservation under Nguyen Minh.

Receptionist: Let me check... Yes, a double room for three nights, checking out on the 15th. Is that correct?

You: That's right.

Receptionist: May I see your passport and a credit card for the deposit?

You: Here you go. Is breakfast included?

Receptionist: Yes, breakfast is served from 7 to 10 in the restaurant on the ground floor. Here's your key card — room 405, fourth floor. The elevator is on your right.

You: Great. What time is check-out?

Receptionist: Check-out is at 11 a.m., but we can arrange a late check-out until 1 p.m. for a small fee.

You: Good to know. Thank you!

Tinh huong 2: Tai quay check-in san bay

Agent: Good morning. Passport and booking reference, please.

You: Here you go. I'm on the 10:30 flight to Singapore.

Agent: Thank you. Would you like a window or aisle seat?

You: Window, please. And can I check this bag?

Agent: Of course. Please place it on the scale... That's 18 kilos, within the limit. Do you have any carry-on?

You: Just this backpack.

Agent: Perfect. Here's your boarding pass. Gate B7, boarding starts at 9:50. Have a good flight!

You: Thank you!

Loi thuong gap cua nguoi Viet

  • Sai: "I want to book a room." (qua truc tiep) → Dung: "I'd like to book a room." (lich su hon)
  • Sai: "How much one night?" → Dung: "How much is it per night?"
  • Sai: "I have booking name Minh." → Dung: "I have a reservation under Minh."
  • Sai: "My luggage is lost. Where is it?" → Dung: "My luggage hasn't arrived. Could you help me locate it?" (lich su va chinh xac hon)
  • Sai: "What time is the plane?" → Dung: "What time does my flight depart?" hoac "What time is my flight?"
  • Sai: "I want window chair." → Dung: "Can I have a window seat?" (seat, khong phai chair)

Meo giao tiep

  • "I'd like..." la cach yeu cau lich su nhat trong moi tinh huong du lich.
  • Luu so booking reference — thuong can khi check-in online hoac tai quay.
  • "Is [X] included?" — cau hoi huu ich: breakfast included? Wi-Fi included? Airport transfer included?
  • Seat vs Chair: Tren may bay/tau/xe = "seat". "Chair" chi dung cho ghe binh thuong.
  • Tip: Luu lich trinh (itinerary) trong dien thoai — de tra cuu nhanh khi can.

Bai tap

1. Ban muon dat phong doi 2 dem. Noi the nao?

I'd like to book a double room for two nights, please.

2. Sua loi: "How much one night?"

How much is it per night?

3. Ban muon ngoi canh cua so tren may bay. Noi the nao?

Can I have a window seat, please?

4. Sua loi: "I have booking name Nguyen."

I have a reservation under Nguyen.

5. May lanh trong phong khong hoat dong. Bao le tan the nao?

Excuse me, the air conditioning in my room isn't working. Could someone take a look?

6. Phan biet "luggage" va "carry-on":
a) Hanh ly ky gui = ___
b) Hanh ly xach tay = ___

a) checked luggage/baggage
b) carry-on (bag/luggage)

7. Ban muon tra phong muon (1 gio chieu thay vi 11 gio sang). Hoi the nao?

Is it possible to get a late check-out? / Can I have a late check-out until 1 p.m.?

8. Chuyen bay bi tre. Hoi nhan vien the nao?

My flight has been delayed. How long is the delay? / When is the new departure time?

9. Dien vao cho trong: "Is breakfast ___ in the room rate?" (bao gom)

included

10. Viet hoi thoai ngan (4-6 cau): ban check-in tai khach san va hoi ve Wi-Fi.

Vi du:
You: Hi, I have a reservation under Tran Lan.
Receptionist: Welcome! Yes, a single room for two nights. Here's your key — room 302.
You: Thank you. Is there Wi-Fi in the room?
Receptionist: Yes, the network is "GrandHotel-Guest" and the password is on the card in your room.
You: Great. And what time is breakfast?
Receptionist: From 6:30 to 9:30 in the lobby restaurant.
