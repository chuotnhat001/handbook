Shopping & Money

Mua sam va tien bac — tu vung va mau cau khi mua hang, tra gia, thanh toan

Mau cau co ban

Hoi gia / Tim san pham

How much is this? — Cai nay bao nhieu?
How much are these? — Nhung cai nay bao nhieu?
Do you have this in [size/color]? — Ban co cai nay mau/size [X] khong?
I'm looking for... — Toi dang tim...
Can I try this on? — Toi thu cai nay duoc khong?
Where's the fitting room? — Phong thu do o dau?

Mua / Quyet dinh

  • I'll take it. — Toi lay cai nay.
  • I'll take two of these. — Toi lay 2 cai nay.
  • I'm just looking, thanks. — Toi chi xem thoi, cam on.
  • I'll think about it. — De toi suy nghi da.
  • Can I get a discount? — Giam gia duoc khong?
  • Is this on sale? — Cai nay dang giam gia khong?

Thanh toan

  • Can I pay by card? — Toi tra bang the duoc khong?
  • Do you accept credit cards? — Ban nhan the tin dung khong?
  • I'll pay in cash. — Toi tra tien mat.
  • Can I have a receipt? — Cho toi hoa don duoc khong?
  • Keep the change. — Giu lai tien thua.

Doi/Tra hang

  • I'd like to return this. — Toi muon tra cai nay.
  • Can I exchange this for a different size? — Toi doi size khac duoc khong?
  • I have the receipt. — Toi co hoa don.
  • What's your return policy? — Chinh sach doi tra the nao?

So sanh voi tieng Viet

Giong: Ca hai ngon ngu deu hoi gia, mac ca, va xac nhan mua tuong tu.

Khac:

  • Tieng Anh dung "How much" cho gia ca (khong dem duoc) va "How many" cho so luong (dem duoc). Tieng Viet dung "bao nhieu" cho ca hai.
  • Tieng Anh phan biet "this" (so it) / "these" (so nhieu). Tieng Viet: "cai nay" / "nhung cai nay".
  • Mac ca o nuoc ngoai khong pho bien nhu o Viet Nam. Chi mac ca o cho troi, garage sale.
  • Tieng Anh noi "I'll take it" (tuong lai) khi quyet dinh mua. Tieng Viet: "Toi lay cai nay" (hien tai).

Dialogue mau

Tinh huong 1: Mua quan ao

Staff: Hi! Can I help you with anything?

You: Yes, I'm looking for a jacket. Something casual.

Staff: Sure! We have these over here. What size are you?

You: I'm a medium. Do you have this one in black?

Staff: Let me check... Yes, here you go. Would you like to try it on?

You: Yes, please. Where's the fitting room?

Staff: Just around the corner on your left.

You: (after trying) It fits perfectly. How much is it?

Staff: It's $89, but it's 20% off today.

You: Great, I'll take it. Can I pay by card?

Staff: Of course! Tap or insert?

Tinh huong 2: Mua sam online — hoi ban

A: I'm thinking of buying a new laptop. Any recommendations?

B: What's your budget?

A: Around $1,000. I mainly use it for coding and some light gaming.

B: Have you looked at the MacBook Air? It's on sale right now — about $999.

A: That's a bit over my budget. Can I find it cheaper somewhere else?

B: Check the refurbished section on Amazon. You can save 15-20% and they still come with a warranty.

A: Good idea! I'll look into it. Thanks!

Loi thuong gap cua nguoi Viet

  • Sai: "How much money is this?" → Dung: "How much is this?" (khong can "money")
  • Sai: "It's too much expensive." → Dung: "It's too expensive." (too + adj, khong them "much")
  • Sai: "Can you cheap?" → Dung: "Can you give me a discount?" (cheap la tinh tu, khong dung lam dong tu)
  • Sai: "I want to buy it more cheap." → Dung: "I want to buy it cheaper." hoac "Can I get it for less?"
  • Sai: "The price is how much?" → Dung: "How much is it?" (How much dung dau cau hoi)
  • Sai: "I can't afford to buy it" (khi chi muon noi dat) → Dung: "It's out of my budget." (lich su hon)

Meo giao tiep

  • "I'm just looking" — cau than ky khi nhan vien hoi ma ban chua muon mua.
  • Hoi gia lich su: "How much is this?" tot hon "What's the price?" (tu nhien hon).
  • Khong mac ca o cua hang co gia niem yet (retail stores). Chi mac ca o flea market, garage sale.
  • "That's a bit over my budget" — cach tu choi lich su khi thay dat.
  • Tip: O My/Anh, khong can tip khi mua sam (chi tip o nha hang, taxi).

Bai tap

1. Dien vao cho trong: "How much ___ these shoes?" (is / are)

are (these shoes = so nhieu)

2. Sua loi: "It's too much expensive for me."

It's too expensive for me.

3. Ban muon thu ao. Noi gi?

Can I try this on? / Where's the fitting room?

4. Dien vao cho trong: "Do you have this ___ a smaller size?" (in / on / at)

in a smaller size

5. Nhan vien hoi "Can I help you?" nhung ban chi muon xem. Tra loi the nao?

I'm just looking, thanks. / No thanks, I'm just browsing.

6. Sua loi: "Can you cheap the price?"

Can you give me a discount? / Can you lower the price?

7. Ban muon tra bang the. Hoi the nao?

Can I pay by card? / Do you accept credit cards?

8. Phan biet "on sale" va "for sale":
a) This house is ___ (dang rao ban)
b) These jeans are ___ — 50% off! (dang giam gia)

a) for sale (dang rao ban)
b) on sale (dang giam gia)

9. Ban mua ao nhung ve nha thay khong vua. Noi gi khi quay lai cua hang?

I'd like to exchange this for a different size. / Can I return this? I have the receipt.

10. Viet hoi thoai ngan (4-6 cau) giua ban va nhan vien ban hang khi ban muon mua mot doi giay.

Vi du:
You: Hi, I'm looking for running shoes.
Staff: What size do you need?
You: Size 42. Do you have anything under $100?
Staff: These are $85 and very popular. Would you like to try them on?
You: Yes, please. (tries) They fit well. I'll take them.
Staff: Great! Cash or card?
