Directions & Transport

Hoi duong va phuong tien — cach chi duong, hoi duong, va su dung giao thong cong cong

Hoi duong

Excuse me, how do I get to [place]?
Could you tell me the way to [place]?
Is there a [place] near here?
How far is it from here?
Can I walk there, or should I take a bus?

Chi duong

  • Go straight — di thang
  • Turn left / Turn right — re trai / re phai
  • Take the first/second left — re trai o nga re thu nhat/thu hai
  • It's on your left/right — no o ben trai/phai ban
  • Go past the [landmark] — di qua [dia diem]
  • It's at the corner — no o goc duong
  • It's across from / opposite — no doi dien voi
  • It's next to / beside — no ben canh
  • It's between [A] and [B] — no nam giua [A] va [B]
  • Go down this street for about 5 minutes — di doc con duong nay khoang 5 phut

Phuong tien giao thong

Gioi tu voi phuong tien

by + phuong tien (khong co "the"): by bus, by train, by car, by taxi, by plane
on + phuong tien cu the: on the bus, on the train, on a plane
in + xe nho (ngoi ben trong): in a car, in a taxi, in an Uber
on foot = di bo (khong noi "by foot" hoac "by walk")

Mau cau ve giao thong

  • How do I get to the airport? — Lam sao den san bay?
  • Which bus goes to [place]? — Xe bus nao di den [noi]?
  • Where's the nearest subway station? — Tram metro gan nhat o dau?
  • How long does it take by bus? — Di bus mat bao lau?
  • Is this the right platform for [destination]? — Day co phai ke dung de di [noi] khong?
  • Does this bus stop at [place]? — Xe nay co dung o [noi] khong?
  • I'd like a one-way/return ticket to [place]. — Toi muon ve mot chieu/khu hoi di [noi].
  • What time is the next train? — Chuyen tau tiep theo may gio?

So sanh voi tieng Viet

Giong: Ca hai ngon ngu deu dung cac cum chi huong (trai, phai, thang) va moc dia diem de chi duong.

Khac:

  • Tieng Anh dung "by + phuong tien" (by bus) — khong co mao tu. Tieng Viet: "bang xe bus" hoac "di xe bus".
  • Tieng Anh phan biet "on" (xe lon) va "in" (xe nho). Tieng Viet dung "tren" cho ca hai.
  • Tieng Anh noi "on foot" (di bo). Tieng Viet: "di bo". Khong noi "by foot" hay "by walk".
  • Tieng Anh dung "take" voi phuong tien: "take a bus/taxi". Tieng Viet: "bat/di taxi".
  • Tieng Anh dung ordinal numbers khi chi duong: "the first left, the second right". Tieng Viet: "nga re thu nhat".

Dialogue mau

Tinh huong 1: Hoi duong tren pho

You: Excuse me, could you tell me how to get to the train station?

Local: Sure! Go straight down this road for about two blocks. Then turn right at the traffic lights.

You: Turn right at the traffic lights. Got it.

Local: Yes, and then it's on your left — you'll see a big sign. It's about a 10-minute walk.

You: Great, thank you! Is there a bus I can take instead?

Local: You can take the number 5 bus from that stop over there. It goes directly to the station.

You: Perfect. Thanks so much!

Tinh huong 2: Mua ve tau

You: Hi, I'd like a return ticket to Manchester, please.

Staff: When are you travelling?

You: Today. What time is the next train?

Staff: The next one leaves at 2:15 from platform 3. It arrives at 4:30.

You: Do I need to transfer?

Staff: No, it's a direct service. That'll be £45.

You: Can I pay by card?

Staff: Of course. Here's your ticket. Platform 3 is down the stairs on your right.

You: Thank you!

Loi thuong gap cua nguoi Viet

  • Sai: "I go to work by walk." → Dung: "I walk to work." hoac "I go to work on foot."
  • Sai: "Turn to the left." → Dung: "Turn left." (khong can "to the")
  • Sai: "I take bus number 5." → Dung: "I take the number 5 bus." (can mao tu "the")
  • Sai: "Go straight about 100 meters." → Dung: "Go straight for about 100 meters." (can "for" truoc khoang cach)
  • Sai: "It's in the left." → Dung: "It's on the/your left." (dung "on" voi left/right)
  • Sai: "How long time does it take?" → Dung: "How long does it take?" (khong can "time")

Meo giao tiep

  • Bat dau bang "Excuse me" khi hoi duong nguoi la — the hien lich su.
  • Nhac lai chi dan de xac nhan: "So I turn right at the lights?" — giup tranh hieu nham.
  • Dung landmarks de mo ta: "It's next to the big supermarket" de hon "It's 200m north".
  • Google Maps la ban tot nhat, nhung biet hoi duong bang tieng Anh van rat huu ich khi mat mang.

Bai tap

1. Dien gioi tu: "I go to work ___ bus every day." (by / on / in)

by bus (by + phuong tien, khong co "the")

2. Sua loi: "I go to school by walk."

I walk to school. / I go to school on foot.

3. Dien gioi tu: "She's sitting ___ the car." (on / in / by)

in the car (xe nho → dung "in")

4. Sap xep lai: "the / turn / at / right / traffic lights"

Turn right at the traffic lights.

5. Ban muon hoi duong den buu dien. Noi the nao?

Excuse me, how do I get to the post office? / Could you tell me the way to the post office?

6. Sua loi: "Go straight about 200 meters."

Go straight for about 200 meters.

7. Dien vao cho trong: "The bank is ___ the supermarket and the pharmacy."

between the supermarket and the pharmacy

8. Ban muon mua ve khu hoi di London. Noi the nao?

I'd like a return ticket to London, please. / Can I get a round-trip ticket to London?

9. Phan biet "on" va "in":
a) I was ___ the train for 2 hours.
b) She's waiting ___ the car.

a) on the train (phuong tien lon)
b) in the car (phuong tien nho)

10. Chi duong tu truong hoc den sieu thi gan nhat (viet 3-4 cau dung: go straight, turn, it's on your...).

Vi du: Go straight for about 200 meters. Turn left at the intersection. Go past the park and take the first right. The supermarket is on your left, next to the bank.
